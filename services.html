History of the Peaceable Kindom Farm

Established in 2004 by Catherine Jameson and Dennis Nater, the Peacable Kingdom was originally a private barn with no boarding. We built our beautiful outdoor arena in 2005 and took on our first boarder in 2008. Our 160X66 indoor arena with sand and rubber footing was completed in March, 2016.
